Raiyan Abrar

Biography

Raiyan Abrar is a designer from Dhaka, Bangladesh specializing in Logo designing and Game Development. He currently studies Computer Science and Engineering in a military based university in Dhaka.

His interest mostly lies on making new things and sharing them to social medias. He loves travelling and taking photographs of his trips a lot.

He also does designing UI/UX for apps, posters for different events, frontend app development and 2d game development.

He made a fan made Pokemon game and was the first in the country to publicly release it through Mazecity, the largest Anime community in Bangladesh.
